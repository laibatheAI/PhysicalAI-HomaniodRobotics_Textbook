## Citation Guidelines

### APA 7th Edition Format

All citations in this textbook follow the APA 7th edition format. Key formatting rules include:

- **Journal Articles**: Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

- **Books**: Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher Name.

- **Conference Papers**: Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings Title* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

- **Technical Reports/Documentation**: Organization Name. (Year). *Title of report*. URL

### In-Text Citations

In-text citations follow the format (Author, Year) for single authors or (Author et al., Year) for multiple authors when there are three or more authors.

### Quality Standards

All citations in this textbook have been verified for accuracy and accessibility. At least 50% of the sources are peer-reviewed academic publications, ensuring the academic rigor required by the project constitution. Each technical claim includes an inline citation, and all data has been cross-referenced before final export.
